Transportation in Pune

  • Metro

    Pune Metro is the 15th metro system in India. It is built and operated by the Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Limited being inaugurated and opened for public on 6 March 2022.

  • Bus

    PMPML is a public transportation provider in Pune which operates bus routes. The PMPML has 519 bus routes in Pune with 7852 bus stops. Their bus routes cover an area from the S.T Stand Lonavala stop to the Varvand Gaon stop and from the Loni Dhamani stop to the Nira Post Office / Shri Buvaji Chowk stop.

  • Airways

    Pune Airport (IATA: PNQ, ICAO: VAPO) is an international airport and an Indian Air Force base serving the city of Pune, Maharashtra, India. It is located at Lohegaon, approximately 11 km (6.8 mi) northeast of the historic centre of Pune.
